About The Organization

Our History

AgroHeirs is a diversified service company, a joint venture of Heirs and Heralds Agro Allied Limited and Scientia, with a specific interest in all-around agricultural production and training. Our core values are sustainability, Innovation, and Collaboration. Birthed in 2024 our inspiring vision is to bring a new dawn on our nation's agricultural sector by building a set of modern farms and farmers equipped with the skill set and mindset to bring this vision to reality. As a sector in this nation, we should be the backbone of the economy. Standing with a united front and equipped to ensure food security in the nation. We would become beams of hope. One of our core driving passion is fulfilling the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals and we are devoted to working hard to play a major role in the areas of No Poverty and Zero Hunger. We aspire to facilitate institutional reforms in the Agricultural sector in line with the continental strategy for education through youth training and development, and entrepreneurship creation. By collaborating with agricultural institutions across the country we can build sustainable and innovative plans and ideas for actualization and practice. .

Our Services

What We Offer to Prospective Learners
1. Cutting-edge training and Capacity Building Agroheirs Hub offers specialized training programs tailored to equip you with the latest techniques in animal science, crop production and management, agricultural extension, agribusiness development, and the application of digital tools in farming.
Our curriculum is designed to provide practical, hands-on experience that will set you apart in the industry.
2. Research and Development (R&D) Opportunities We collaborate with graduates, students, universities, and research institutions to turn academic projects into impactful solutions.
Whether you are developing innovative feed formulas, animal health solutions, or sustainable farming practices, Agroheirs Hub offers the support you need to bring your ideas to life.
3. Entrepreneurship Development and Mentorship Our entrepreneurship programs are designed to guide you through the journey of creating, developing, and scaling your agribusiness. We provide mentorship from industry leaders, access to funding, and strategic partnerships to help you establish a successful business.
4. Commercialization and Market Access: We support you in transitioning your research projects into viable market products. Our team will assist you in product development, business model design, and go-to-market strategies, ensuring that your innovation reaches the right audience.
