Who Are We?

AgroHeirs is a diversified service company that is a joint venture of Heirs and Heralds Agro Allied Limited and Scientia, with a specific interest in all-around agricultural production and training with our core values being sustainability, Innovation, and Collaboration. Birthed in 2024 our inspiring vision is to bring a new dawn on the agricultural sector of our nation by building a set of modern farms and farmers equipped with the skill set and mindset to bring this vision to reality. As a sector in this nation, we should be the backbone of the economy. Standing with a united front and equipped to ensure food security in the nation. We would become beams of hope. One of our core driving passion is fulfilling the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals and we are devoted to working hard to play a major role in the areas of No Poverty and Zero Hunger. We aspire to facilitate institutional reforms in the Agricultural sector in line with the continental strategy for education through youth training and development, and entrepreneurship creation. By collaborating with agricultural institutions across the country we can build sustainable and innovative plans and ideas for actualization and practice.

Our Aim and Objectives?

1. To take students and interested individuals to the farm for practical learning sessions, allowing them to gain hands-on agricultural experience and innovative sustainable ideas by collaborating with agricultural institutions across Nigeria.

2. To provide a diversified service that distributes farm produce, raises livestock, provides innovative solutions, and impacts the next generation of modern farmers.

3. To become an integral part of making a major contribution to the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals, specifically in the areas of No Poverty and Zero Hunger.

Why Join Agroheirs Hub?

1. Access to Modern Agricultural Training Learn from industry experts and seasoned practitioners through workshops, boot camps, and mentorship sessions.

2. Exposure to Industry Networks and Opportunities Connect with top industry professionals, investors, and fellow agripreneurs. Gain exposure to a wide range of opportunities, from internships and research grants to business partnerships and funding.

3. Be Part of a Nationwide Community Agroheirs Hub aims to build a dynamic community of forward-thinking agripreneurs that spans across states and institutions. By joining us, you become part of a movement that is shaping the future of agriculture in Nigeria and beyond.
